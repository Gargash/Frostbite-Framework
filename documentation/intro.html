What is Frostbite Framework?

Frostbite Framework is a light weight MVC framework, based off of codeignitor. I created this project for fun, and it really had my brain going. I love a challenge and in my personal opinion, I feel this was the hardest, yet the most fun thing i have ever made.

So what is the difference between CI and Frostbite? Well for starts, Frostbite offers even faster page loading. One of the things i dislike about CI is how much code they have to use to load a single page, And that doesnt include all the time it takes to load the additional resources one might need to use. Now, Dont get me wrong, CI is a very fast framework, Faster then most, and its has very clean, and documented code. I also feel that CI just has "too much" stuff in it. Yes its nice to have resources, but i also believe that the people building on a framework should have enough know how to make those resources themselves, when they need it, which brings me to my third point. Frostbite has very little, and extendable code. The code is very easy to understand and follow.
